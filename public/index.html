Welcome to Fernando's

A home away from home in the heart of PE

Tucked behind a charming Victorian facade on Cape Road, Fernando's Guest House & Grill has been welcoming travellers, families and business visitors to Port Elizabeth for years. With a reputation for generous hospitality, secure grounds and a grill the locals love, it's the kind of place guests return to again and again.

Self-Catering Suites

Full kitchens, lounges and private bedrooms — perfect for families, business stays and longer visits.

On-Site Grill

Fernando's famous grill — breakfast, lunch and dinner favourites served daily for guests and walk-ins.

Secure Parking

Gated, on-site parking so you can rest easy knowing your vehicle is safe throughout your stay.

Central Location

On Cape Road in Mill Park — minutes from the beachfront, hospitals, university and major attractions.

Welcome to Fernando's

Where Victorian Charm Meets Modern Comfort

Set in two beautiful Victorian houses on Cape Road, Fernando's has been welcoming travellers to Port Elizabeth for over 35 years. Whether you're here for business, a family holiday, or a quiet weekend, our spacious suites and warm hospitality make every stay feel like home.

Self-Catering Suites

Full kitchens, lounges, and private entrances. Perfect for families, business stays, and longer visits.

On-Site Restaurant

Fernando's Grill serves home-style meals and grills — open to guests and walk-ins alike.

Central Location

Minutes from the beachfront, boardwalk, Greenacres, and central PE. Secure on-site parking.

Friendly Hosts

Family-run for over three decades. We know PE, we'll give you the local tips, and we'll remember your name.
