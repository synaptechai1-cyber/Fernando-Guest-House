Our Story

Port Elizabeth's
first guest house

Built in 1902, Fernando's is more than a place to sleep — it's a living piece of Eastern Cape history, lovingly maintained for over a century.

Nestled in the exclusive garden suburb of Mill Park, our Victorian-style property enchants guests with original pressed-steel ceilings, ornate fireplaces, yellow-wood floors, and the kind of warmth that only a family-run establishment can offer.

Contact Astrud or Michelle — our dedicated hosts who treat every guest like family, and who have helped thousands of travellers feel at home in Nelson Mandela Bay.
