Our Story

Port Elizabeth's
first guest house

Welcome to Fernando's Guest House & Grill — a family-owned Victorian gem nestled in the exclusive garden suburb of Mill Park, Nelson Mandela Bay.

The house itself dates back to 1902, and we proudly opened our doors as Port Elizabeth's very first guest house in 1983. Since then, we've been collecting beautiful pieces of antique furniture and original art — and building a reputation for warmth that no chain hotel can match.

The guest house spans three Victorian houses in close proximity to each other, all brimming with character: original pressed-steel ceilings, ornate cast-iron fireplaces, gleaming yellowwood floors, and the kind of charm that only a century-old building can offer with a modern touch where it's needed.

Contact Astrud or Michelle directly for bookings — we respond personally and promptly to every enquiry.

🌱

Eco-Friendly: We've installed solar geysers and photovoltaic panels, collect rainwater, compost our waste, and grow our own organic herbs and vegetables on site.

Directions from N2

  1. Take the Settlers Way off-ramp
  2. Follow the M4 — look for the Albany Road turn-off and take it
  3. Cross through 2 sets of traffic lights
  4. At the third traffic light turn right into Cape Road
  5. Fernando's is the third house on the left
